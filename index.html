It's time to change something



The Challenge

The Greek population has faced a dilemma in the past few months that has worsened the personal and financial situation of many. Your job is it to find solutions through Bitcoin and the Blockchain that solve fundamental problems in Greece and other countries.


Coding Category

In the coding category, you have to develop a functional prototype that is able to present your intended solution to the judges.


Thinking Category

The thinking category is for those who are not studying Computer Science or Informatics. In this category you will have to create a feasible business presentation of the challenge we presented. No code, pure presentation.


What you can expect

Lots of Fun

The main goal of this Hackathon is it to offer a new, amazing and fun experience for everyone involved. This Hackathon will surely deliver!

Learn new things

You will be introduced to new technologies, API's, concepts and ideas. With this you will be able to come up with innovative applications that solve fundamental problems.

Code away

As the main purpose of the Hackathon is it to implement and develop new applications, you can be assured that a lot of coding will happen!

New Ideas

Every Hackathon comes with a Challenge, where you have to come up and propose a solution. The Hackathon is there to foster these ideas and help you execute them.

Meet new people

People all over Greece and even the World will be attending the Hackathon in Athens. You will get to know like-minded people and potentially make new friends!

Lots of Coffee

The Hackathon will last for 3 days and it will require a lot of effort from you to come up with and implement your desired application. What better help for execution is there than coffee?

Frequently Asked Questions

What about food and beverages?

All provided for and free! This means that during the time of the Hackathon (Friday - Sunday) you will be getting free and healthy food. But we will of course also get some Pizza, Red Bull and lots of Coffee.

If I'm not from Athens, where can I sleep?

For accommodation you will have to find a place yourself, sadly. We are currently working to get the money so that we can at least cover part of your travel and accommodation expenses.

What if I'm alone?

Not a problem at all! Many people will come to the Hackathon alone and you can perhaps team up with these people and work together on the application. You can also work and present your solution alone.
